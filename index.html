What Our Customers Say

Dzaky Faisa

Regular Customer

"A post-industrial style cafe, just 150m from Fatmawati MRT in South Jakarta's Cilandak District. Set in a large house's backyard, it offers a cozy indoor space and an outdoor area ideal for gatherings. The well-brewed coffee and attentive owner add to the experience."

Krishna Astu

Regular Customer

"The ample parking initially drew me here, as few Jakarta cafes offer such space. The building resembles an old house, so I got a bit lost, but was delighted to find a cool garden for enjoying afternoon coffee. The coffee, around 30k, tasted nice, and the french fries snack was satisfying."

Nabilah Rahmagitha

New Customer

"Just a 4-minute walk from Fatmawati MRT, I visited early in the morning when it was still quiet. The cafe offers a cozy indoor area and a spacious outdoor space. I tried the Berrycious drink, which was refreshingly sweet and perfect to start the day."
